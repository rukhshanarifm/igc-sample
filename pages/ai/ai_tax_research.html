  • Low Tax-to-GDP Ratio (8.6%): Among lowest globally, limiting development spending and fiscal sustainability
  • Narrow Tax Base: Only 3.2M active filers in a population of 230M; vast informal economy remains untaxed
  • Heavy Reliance on Indirect Taxes (71%): Regressive tax structure burdens lower-income groups disproportionately
  • Tax Expenditure (‚Ç®1.5 trillion): Exemptions and concessions erode revenue base by ~4% of GDP annually
  • Weak Compliance & Enforcement: High evasion rates, particularly in real estate, retail, and services sectors

Addressing these structural weaknesses requires understanding their root causes through rigorous academic research.
